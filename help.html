Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started

Quick guides to help you begin using Smart Aid.

How do I sign up as a Donor?

Click Donor on the role selection page, then fill out the sign-up form with your name, contact, and donor preferences. After signing up you can post donations that nearby volunteers can accept.

How do I report someone in need?

Choose "Reporter / Community Leader" from the role selection page, sign up and verify your contact. Use the "Report Donation" or "Report Need" form on your dashboard to submit location, description, and any photos. Our system will match nearby donors.

What can I donate?

Common donations include surplus cooked food (properly sealed), packaged groceries, clothing, blankets, and small household items. Avoid expired food or unsafe items. For large/complex donations, contact support to coordinate pickup.

Safety & Verification

Guidelines to keep donations safe and trustworthy.

How does verification work?

We encourage reporters and donors to verify contact details (phone/email). For trusted community leaders, we may run additional checks. Always meet in public places when possible and document items with clear photos.

Are donated food items checked for safety?

Smart Aid provides guidance only. Donors should ensure food is safe, properly packaged, and within recommended storage times. If you're unsure, mark the donation clearly or contact support for advice.

Troubleshooting

Common technical issues and solutions.

I can't log in — what should I do?

Try resetting your password via the login page. If you don't receive the reset email, check spam/junk folders. Still stuck? Contact support with your registered email and a brief description.

My location isn't accurate on reports.

Make sure your device's location services are enabled and the browser has permission to access location. You can also manually type an address when reporting a need.

Can't find an answer? Use the contact options on the right, or email support@smart-aid.example (replace with your real support address).
